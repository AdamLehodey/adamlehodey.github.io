Confident and exacting, I am driven by a desire to learn, explore, and deliver meaningful change. Currently working for a Member of Parliament in the House of Commons, I have a deep practical and technical understanding of British and international politics. I will consolidate this and explore a wide number of other fields (philosophy, economics, history, etc) as an incoming scholar to a unique Dual-BA Programme between SciencesPo Collège Universitaire and Columbia University in New York. Además, me encantan los idiomas; I’m fluent in French, English and Spanish, and am learning Italian. For any business or personal enquiries, please reach out via the ‘contact me’ page.

Education is important, vital even. Man’s mind is the ultimate source of wealth. But life is also an adventure. I’ve travelled extensively throughout Europe and North America, with South America and the middle east next on my list. One of my greatest inspirations for the essays and articles I write on my blog are conversations I’ve had with friends and others I’ve met on my journeys. My motivation to write is to share and develop my own ideas, to start a conversation, and because prose is inherently beautiful. If you’re interested in ideas on subjects as varied as philosophy to urbanism, check out my ‘essays and articles’ page now. -Adam
