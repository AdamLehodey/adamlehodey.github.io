University Admissions Tutoring

Applying for university can be daunting. It's also become increasingly competitive to get into top instutitions - with many having had many hours of practice writing personal statements and doing interviews, as well as fierce competition from abroad.

Having received offers from a number of prestigious universities (University College London, the LSE, Durham University, Warwick, King's College London, and SciencesPo and Columbia University in the US, where I have chosen to study), I know what it takes to write a strong personal statement and to secure a place at a top Russell Group or Ivy League institution. I will guide you through the whole process: the 'super-curricular' activities you should be doing to enhance your application, deadlines to keep in mind, I will assist with helping you choose the right course, and of course, I'll guide you through every step of writing your personal statement.

I will then provide mock-interviews and ensure you have everything you need to succeed. This is an investment that will be well worth it and will help you get way ahead of your peers.
